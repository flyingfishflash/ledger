Welcome to your general ledger.

You can add accounts and transactions to the general ledger. Transactions are added in accordance to the double-entry bookkeeping system, where every transaction has to be recorded twice, once as a debit entry and once as a credit entry.

Accounts can be deleted if they have no transactions. Transactions can be deleted or updated. When a transaction is deleted or updated then both corresponding entries are modified as well.

You can also view the trial balance, which is a statement of all credits and debits in the general ledger. You can view the general ledger and trial balance in the application and you can export them to a .csv file.
